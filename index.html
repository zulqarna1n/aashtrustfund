Our Mission

Aash's Trust Fund exists to help stray cats survive, heal, and live safely. Every donation and volunteer effort contributes to this cause. We focus on rescue, medical treatment, short-term shelter and adoption support.

What we do

  • Daily feeding and monitoring
  • Rescue and emergency medical care
  • Vaccination and TNR where possible
  • Temporary shelter and adoption assistance

How to Help

Donate, volunteer, or share our mission. Small monthly contributions make a large impact when pooled together.
